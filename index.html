About Pauline

With a Franco-Australian background and deep local knowledge of Nontron and the Dordogne region, Pauline offers personalized assistance services to the English-speaking community.

Having navigated the complexities of French administration and local services herself, Pauline understands the challenges faced by English speakers in the region and is passionate about making life easier for expats and visitors.

Fluent in both English and French, Pauline bridges the language gap to help you navigate local systems, communicate effectively, and enjoy your time in this beautiful region of France.
